Kipros Lofitis
I have a PhD in Philosophy from the University of Nottingham. My main research interest is metaethics, and I am also interested in several other topics including applied ethics and the philosophy of religion. I have previously taught at the University of Cyprus and the University of Nicosia. I am enthusiastic about teaching.
